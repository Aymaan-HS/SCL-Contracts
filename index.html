About SCL

ENGINEERED FOR EXCELLENCE

SCL Contracts Pvt. Ltd. is a fully owned subsidiary of Siddhika Coatings Limited — India's largest single-brand texture paint contract company, listed on the NSE SME segment. With over 20 years of history in architectural coatings and construction, our group brings deep expertise to every project.

Our business philosophy always revolved around customer satisfaction and retention. SCL Contracts provides a satisfying experience combining the best services and products — from facade renovation and waterproofing to design-build and structural retrofitting.

We are the authorised marketing partner for SK Kaken Ltd. (SKK), Japan, with over 20 years of association. Almost 60% of our business comes from repeat clients — a testament to the trust we have built.
